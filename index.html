About Samdanest

Samdanest Nigeria Ltd specialises in Livestock and animal feed production. Our range of products include, Eggs, Old Layers, Cockerels, Broilers,Breeder Birds, Pigs as well as livestock manure.Our Subsidiary company Samdanest Feeds deals with the production of animal feeds, such as Grower mash, Layer mash, pelleted fish meal as well as pig feed.
